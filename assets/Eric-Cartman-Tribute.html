Eric Cartman

The best South Park character!

Eric Cartman is hands down the funniest character in the animated TV series South Park. His witty comments, ignorance to certain topics, and "I-get-what-I-want" attitude are among some of his best qualities that make him the most laughable character in South Park.

    Examples in episodes:
  • Season 4 Episode 5 - Cartman Joins NAMBLA
    Cartman decides to seek friendship using the Internet, and finds an older man who is more than willing to be his friend...and more.

  • Season 5 Episode 4 - Scott Tenorman Must Die
    After the other boys explain to Cartman that pubes don't count unless you grow your own, Cartman realizes he's been tricked by the school bully, Scott Tenorman.

  • Season 5 Episode 6 - Cartmanland
    Cartman inherits a million dollars from his grandmother and fulfills his lifelong dream of owning his own amusement park: Cartmanland!

  • Season 7 Episode 11 - Casa Bonita
    Kyle chooses Stan, Kenny and Butters to celebrate his birthday at Casa Bonita. When Cartman finds out he's not invited, he arranges for Butters to conveniently go "missing."

  • Season 8 Episode 5 - Awesom-O
    Cartman plans to learn all of Butters' innermost secrets and then use them against him, by pretending to be Butters' new best friend, a robot named AWESOM-O.

  • Season 8 Episode 13 - Cartman's Incredible Gift
    After sustaining a severe head injury, Cartman appears to have the power to see into the future. South Park detectives are quick to enlist his help in cracking unsolved murder cases.

  • Season 10 Episodes 12 & 13 (Two part episode) - Go God Go and Go God Go XII
    Eric Cartman has frozen himself in an attempt to make his three-week wait for a Nintendo Wii pass quickly.

  • Season 13 Episode 2 - The Coon
    "The Coon" rises from the trash and takes his place as a lone vigilante who wipes out crime in the town of South Park.

  • Season 14 Episode 3 - Medicinal Fried Chicken
    Cartman's favorite restaurant has been shut down and replaced by a store that sells medicinal marijuana.
